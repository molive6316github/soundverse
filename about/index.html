Who

molive6316 — the person behind MXT Apex and Soundverse. More producer than performer, more architect than narrator, except when it's the other way around.

The music exists because there was no other good way to make the universe legible. The characters exist because the music needed somewhere to live.

What Soundverse Is

A music project with a mythology attached. Not a concept album in the traditional sense — more like a universe that happens to express itself primarily through sound.

The site is the universe's interface. The music is its primary text. The lore pages are footnotes — useful for context, never more important than the thing they annotate.

Nothing here is finished. That's a feature.

How This Was Made

The aesthetic comes from a specific set of constraints: no frameworks, muted everything, a single accent color, and the belief that cinematic atmosphere is a function of restraint — not complexity.

Cormorant Garamond for the things that should feel old and certain. DM Mono for everything that needs to feel slightly technical, slightly cold. Grain overlay because nothing digital should feel too clean.
