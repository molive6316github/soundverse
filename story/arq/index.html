Biography

Arq is not defined by origin. The question of where they came from is less useful than the question of what they leave behind — and what they leave behind is always a frequency that other things start to orbit.

They exist in motion. Not restlessness — precision. Each departure is a calculation. Each arrival is already the beginning of the next exit. This is not tragedy. Arq would correct you on that.

The blue-green eyes are the only fixed point. Everything else about the body — its weight, its outline, its certainty — fluctuates. The eyes stay.

Design Notes

Palette Muted neutrals throughout — no saturation except the eyes
Eyes #5b9ea0 — the only color that belongs to them
Silhouette Ambiguous — readable as any gender, any era
Expression Neutral to the point of intensity. Not cold. Waiting.
Reference mood Late-night highway. Long exposure photography.

Cuber once asked Arq why they always leave before the thing is finished. Arq said: "The frequency doesn't stop because I'm gone. It just stops being yours to hear." Cuber hasn't asked again.

Role in the Music

Arq is the primary voice of Soundverse — the perspective most of the music is narrated from or directed at. When a track references "I," it's usually Arq. When it references "you," that's almost never the listener.

The music doesn't explain Arq; it demonstrates them. Each song is a behavior, not a description. You learn the character the way you learn a person — by accumulation, by noticing what they keep returning to.
