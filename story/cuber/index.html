Biography (Placeholder)

Cuber exists in direct contrast to Arq — where Arq is motion, Cuber is structure. Where Arq leaves, Cuber remains. The relationship between them is the central tension of the Origin Arc: what does it mean to build something for someone who was never going to stay?

Cuber builds anyway. That's the character — not naivety, not denial, but a philosophical position. The act of building is its own justification.
